Tribute on Umar Musa Yar'Adua

Umaru Musa Yar’Adua

Nigerian politician who was the President of Nigeria from 2007 to 2010

Timeline of Umaru Musa Yar’Adua life:

  • 1951 - Born in Katsina, Nigera
  • 1958 - He started his education at Rafukka Primary School
  • 1965 - He attended the Government College at Keffi until 1969.
  • 1971 - He received a Higher School Certificate from Barewa College
  • 1972 - attended Ahmadu Bello University in Zaria from 1972 to 1975, where he obtained a B.Sc. degree in Education and Chemistry, and then returned in 1978 to pursue an M.Sc. degree in Analytical Chemistry.
  • 1978 - He pursue his M.Sc. degree in Analytical Chemistry at Ahmadu Bello University in Zaria.
  • 1988 - He was a member of the Constituent Assembly.
  • 1991 - He was a member of the party’s National Caucus and the SDP State Secretary in Katsina and contested the Governorship election, but lost to Saidu Barda.
  • 2000 - During his administration as governor, Katsina became the fifth northern Nigerian state to adopt sharia, or Islamic law.
  • 2007 - He was annouced as Nigeria President.
  • 2009 - He left for Saudi Arabia to receive treatment for pericarditis.
  • 2010 - He was pronounced dead on the 5th of May, 2010.
